## Search
`GET /api/v1/stickers/search`
Search for stickers by keyword.

## Recommended
`GET /api/v1/stickers/recommended`
Get a list of recommended stickers.

## Related by ID
`GET /api/v1/stickers/[id]/related`
Get stickers related to a specific sticker ID.
